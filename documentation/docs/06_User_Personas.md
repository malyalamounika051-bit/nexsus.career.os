# User Personas: Nexus Career OS

## Purpose
Brings user research to life through two distinct, concrete target personas.

---

### Persona 1: Mounika (The Graduating Tech Student)
- **Profile**: 21 years old, graduating with a B.Tech in Computer Science. Focuses on frontend development.
- **Goal**: Tailor resumes for competitive React and Frontend roles. Highlight academic projects and coding club achievements.
- **Frustrations**:
  - Feels overwhelmed trying to match different job specifications.
  - Doesn't know what skills recruiters look for in React roles.
- **How Nexus Career OS helps**:
  - Analyzes her projects to auto-generate ATS bullet points.
  - Shows an instant skill gap report (e.g., matching React/JS, but flagging TypeScript as missing).

---

### Persona 2: Rohit (The Self-Taught Switcher)
- **Profile**: 28 years old, transitioning from Operations to DevOps engineering.
- **Goal**: Align operations experience with infrastructure concepts (CI/CD, Docker).
- **Frustrations**:
  - Resumes look too operational; lacks formal tech credentials.
  - Rejection from ATS screeners because of missing keyword density.
- **How Nexus Career OS helps**:
  - Translates operational achievements into clean STAR method resume descriptions.
  - Recommends targeted learning steps for missing AWS and Terraform concepts.
