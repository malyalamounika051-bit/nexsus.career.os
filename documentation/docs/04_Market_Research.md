# Market Research: Tech Hiring & Resume Automation

## Purpose
Examines the size, segments, and competitor space of the career technology market.

## Market Gaps
Existing solutions are highly fragmented:
- **Resume Builders (e.g., Resume.io, Novoresume)**: Provide beautiful templates, but lack AI tailoring and mock interview syncing.
- **Skill Assessment Builders**: Measure user knowledge but don't connect directly back to the active resume or roadmap.
- **Career GPS Guides**: Provide static path info without evaluating individual profile gaps dynamically.
