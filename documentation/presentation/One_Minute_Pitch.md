# One-Minute Pitch Script

## Purpose
A structured talk track for a 60-second presentation or video intro.

## Script & Timing

### 00:00 - 00:15: The Hook
"Searching for a junior tech job is incredibly repetitive. Candidates waste hours custom-tailoring resume bullet points, guessing what ATS keywords they need, and applying blindly."

### 00:15 - 00:40: The Solution
"That is why we built **Nexus Career OS**. A unified workspace where a candidate's Master Profile is the single source of truth. With one click, our AI matches their profile against any job description, tailors the resume summary, rewrites projects using the STAR method, and identifies missing skills in real-time."

### 00:40 - 00:60: The Value
"We replace manual resume tailoring and feedback loops with a streamlined, database-backed platform. Nexus Career OS is how the modern tech workforce prepares, applies, and lands their first developer roles."
