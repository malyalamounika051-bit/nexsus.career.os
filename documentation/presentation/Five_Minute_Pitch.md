# Five-Minute Pitch Deck Script

## Purpose
A comprehensive, slide-by-slide presentation script for the final demo session.

---

### Slide 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Title**: Nexus Career OS: The Unified Copilot for Tech Job Seekers.
- **Script**: "Good afternoon. If you have applied for tech roles recently, you know the frustration. The process is broken. Job seekers are forced to maintain dozens of resume versions, guessing what ATS screeners want. We are introducing Nexus Career OS..."

---

### Slide 2: Core Product Value (0:45 - 2:00)
- **Title**: The Master Profile Source of Truth.
- **Script**: "Nexus consolidates everything. A candidate fills out their profile once. This database-backed schema becomes the single source of truth that feeds all downstream modules—from resume builder, to interview coaching, to skill gap analyzer. No duplicate data entry."

---

### Slide 3: AI Resume & Gap Engine (2:00 - 3:15)
- **Title**: Real-time Tailoring & Skills Matching.
- **Script**: "By pasting a job description, our AI tailors summaries and experience bullet points in seconds. It compares requirements to the profile, highlighting matching vs missing skills in real-time, giving the user a clear list of what to learn next."

---

### Slide 4: Business Strategy & Growth (3:15 - 4:15)
- **Title**: B2C Freemium & B2B University Licensing.
- **Script**: "We monetize through a B2C premium model for advanced resume tailoring and SARA co-pilot interview sessions. We are also pursuing B2B licensing partnerships with universities and coding bootcamps to reach graduating developers at scale."
