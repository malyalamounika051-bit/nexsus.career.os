# Pitch Deck Presentation: Nexus Career OS

## Purpose
Slide-by-slide guide for the 5-minute hackathon presentation, including talk tracks and visual cues.

---

### Slide 1: Title (The Hook)
- **Visuals**: Modern Glassmorphic Logo with slogan: "The AI Career Co-pilot that Lands the Job."
- **Talk Track**: "Good morning judges. We've all seen how painful the entry-level job hunt is. Today, we are presenting Nexus Career OS..."

---

### Slide 2: The Problem (The Friction)
- **Visuals**: Flowchart illustrating a candidate manually copy-pasting their details on multiple applications, only to get filtered out by ATS parsers.
- **Talk Track**: "Job seekers spend hours copy-pasting profiles into customized resume formats. Up to 75% are filtered out by ATS screeners because of minor keyword mismatches."

---

### Slide 3: The Solution (The Single Truth)
- **Visuals**: A central Master Profile that auto-populates downstream Resume Builders, Mock Interviews, and Skill Gap reports.
- **Talk Track**: "Nexus introduces a unified workspace. By keeping a single verified profile as the source of truth, our AI tailors resumes for any job description instantly."

---

### Slide 4: Real-time Demo
- **Visuals**: Screencast of profile-driven resume generation and the interactive skill gap analyzer.
- **Talk Track**: "Notice how we paste a job description, and the system tailors the summary using only the candidate's verified skills. We get an instant ATS compatibility score."
